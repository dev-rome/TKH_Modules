About Me

My name is Jerome Haynes. I'm 30 years old, my love for technology started at an early age. My interest in learning technology came from my older brother, my love for video games, and my mentor Ornell. Growing up, I would always watch my brother code using python and java software. He then broke down how the web works and even how the websites and games I played would not exist without technology. As time went on, I was introduced to my mentor Ornell through a mutual friend. He is a full-stack software engineer. Observing him make applications, websites, debugging, working with APIs and databases drove my interest in the tech world.
